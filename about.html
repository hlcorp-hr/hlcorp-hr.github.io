About HLCorp HR

Transforming careers and businesses across Thailand

Who We Are

HLCorp HR is Thailand's leading recruitment and human resources solutions provider, dedicated to connecting exceptional talent with outstanding opportunities across the kingdom.

Founded with a vision to revolutionize the recruitment landscape in Thailand, we have grown to become the trusted partner for both job seekers and employers. Our team of experienced HR professionals understands the unique dynamics of the Thai job market and leverages cutting-edge technology to deliver results.

With offices in Bangkok, Chiang Mai, and Phuket, we serve clients nationwide, offering comprehensive recruitment services, HR consulting, and talent management solutions.
